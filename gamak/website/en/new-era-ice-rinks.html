Digital Transformation of Ice Rinks

An ice rink that attracts crowds and pays for itself

The Arctic LED system transforms an ice rink into an interactive multimedia arena. Three elements — LED mesh in the ice surface, screens on the boards, and illuminated RGB handrails — work together to create a unique visual environment.

The operator controls everything from a phone, tablet, or laptop. Colors, animations, and advertising content are changed in real time. The ice rink becomes a platform for light shows, sponsor advertising, and themed events — and each of these applications generates additional revenue.

Three System Components

What is included in the Arctic LED system?

Each element works independently, but together they create a synchronized visual environment covering the ice surface, boards, and rink perimeter.

LED RGB Mesh

LED pixels installed beneath the ice surface. Create dynamic patterns, animations, and logos visible through the ice. Interactive games, light shows, sponsor branding.

Details

LED Screens on Boards

Details

LED RGB Handrails

Digital LED handrails mounted on top of the boards. Dynamic colors with adjustable speed, intensity, and effects. Controlled from a phone via Wi-Fi and Bluetooth.

LED RGB Mesh

Interactive lighting in the ice surface

The LED Pixel Mesh is installed beneath the ice surface and creates dynamic patterns, animations, and logos visible through the ice. Skaters glide over a glowing surface — an experience that attracts crowds and sets the rink apart from the competition.

The system supports interactive games, light shows synchronized with music, and sponsor logo display. Controlled through professional VJ software using DDP, sACN, and ArtNet protocols.

LED Screens on Boards

LED Screens — advertising and video content on rink boards

LED screens mounted on ice rink boards replace traditional advertising banners with dynamic video content. They display sponsor logos, live match scores, animations, and messages for spectators.

Screens are visible from both the stands and TV cameras — an advertising platform generating revenue from every game, training session, and event at the rink. The system can be synchronized with the LED Mesh and RGB handrails to create a cohesive visual environment.

Digital LED RGB Handrails

LED RGB Handrails — dynamic perimeter lighting for ice rinks

Digital LED RGB handrails mounted on top of ice rink boards. Display dynamic colors with adjustable speed, intensity, and effects. Polycarbonate profile resistant to moisture, weather conditions, and mechanical impacts — designed for ice rinks, roller parks, and ice slides.

Controlled via a web interface on a phone, tablet, or laptop. Pre-programmed animations with customization options. Optional audio-reactive mode — lighting synchronized with music. Compatible with DDP and ArtNet protocols for advanced installations.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Arctic LED system for ice rinks?

The Arctic LED system is a set of three products for the digital transformation of ice rinks: LED RGB Mesh installed under the ice (animations on the surface), LED RGB handrails on the boards (dynamic perimeter lighting), and LED screens on the boards (displaying ads and video content). All elements can be synchronized and controlled from a single panel.

Is the LED RGB Mesh safe under the ice?

Yes. LED Mesh modules have IP65 waterproof rating, 12 V operating voltage (safe voltage), and capsules filled with adhesive for durability. The elements are designed for ice rink conditions — humidity, low temperatures, and mechanical load from skaters.

What ice rink area can the LED Mesh cover?

One control box handles a section up to 4 meters wide and 40 meters long (8 modules of 1 m × 10 m). Sections can be combined to cover the entire ice surface — from small recreational facilities to full-size 30 × 60 m rinks.

How is the LED lighting controlled on the ice rink?

LED RGB handrails are controlled via a web interface on any device (phone, tablet, laptop) using Wi-Fi and Bluetooth. LED Mesh and screens support professional protocols: DDP, sACN, and ArtNet. Pre-programmed animations are available with customization options. Optionally — audio-reactive mode synchronizing lighting with music.

Does LED lighting generate additional revenue?

Yes. LED screens and Mesh display sponsor logos and messages, opening a new revenue stream from advertising. LED handrails and ice animations attract more visitors — an ice rink with light effects generates higher attendance and longer visitor stays.

Can the Arctic LED system be installed on an existing ice rink?

Yes. LED RGB handrails are mounted modularly on existing boards (sliding system). LED Mesh is installed in the ice during the freezing process. LED screens are attached to the boards. The system does not require facility reconstruction — it is an upgrade to existing infrastructure.
