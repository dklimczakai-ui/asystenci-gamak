Eligible Applicants

Who Can Apply?

The program is aimed at three categories of applicants. Each type of entity has different formal requirements.

Public Finance Sector Units

Local government units (municipalities, districts, voivodeships), municipal sports centers (MOSiR), sports and recreation centers. The main group of program beneficiaries.

Associations and Foundations

Non-profit organizations with a sports profile, established before December 31, 2022. Statutory activity in the field of physical culture is required.

Commercial Law Companies

Companies established by local government units, where the sole owner is a municipality. 100% municipal ownership structure is required.

Important Restrictions

What Does the Program Not Fund?

Not all expenses are eligible. Before submitting an application, make sure your investment does not include the following items.

✗ Land purchase
✗ Gastronomy and catering facilities
✗ SPA and wellness zones
✗ Injury-prone surfaces (asphalt, cinder)
✗ Spectator stands and bleachers
✗ Office spaces
✗ Outdoor swimming pools

Post-Grant Obligations

Beneficiary Obligations

After receiving funding, the beneficiary is required to meet a number of requirements throughout the facility's durability period.

  • Facility durability: 10 years — the facility must be maintained and operated for a minimum of 10 years from final acceptance. Change of purpose requires MSiT approval.
  • Public accessibility — the facility must be accessible to all residents. It cannot serve exclusively one group (e.g., a single sports club).
  • Bicycle racks — a minimum of 3 U-type bicycle racks per investment task. This is a formal requirement at final inspection.
  • Photographic documentation — for modernization projects, 5-10 photos of the existing condition (before works begin) are required as part of the application.
  • Construction start no later than 2027 — construction works must begin no later than 2027. The implementation schedule is part of the application evaluation.
