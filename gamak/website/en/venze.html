Engineering

Technology That Pays for Itself

Four key systems that set VENZE apart from the competition. Each designed with ice rink operators in mind.

V-LOCK BUCKLES

Patented buckle system that cannot be "over-turned." Ultra-HD Polymer instead of standard plastic. 80% reduction in service costs compared to standard solutions.

DRY-TECH LINER

Replaceable liners that dry in record time — approximately 15 minutes with forced air circulation. Your customers will never receive a damp boot.

EASY-SIZE SYSTEM

Clear, non-erasable size markings on the heel and tongue with a color-coded system. Lightning-fast equipment issue even at full capacity — 45 seconds per customer.

STEEL-HD BLADE

High-density hardened steel. Requires sharpening 3x less often than standard recreational blades. Less wear = lower operating costs throughout the season.
