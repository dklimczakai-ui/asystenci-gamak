§7 Cookie Policy

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on the user's device (computer, tablet, smartphone) when using a website. They are used for the proper functioning of the site, remembering user preferences, and traffic analysis.

What cookies do we use?

  • Essential (technical) — ensure the proper functioning of the site, remember cookie consent. User consent is not required.
  • Analytical — collect anonymous information about how the site is used (Google Analytics 4). Help us understand which content is most popular and how users navigate the site.
  • Marketing — used to display personalized ads and measure campaign effectiveness. Used only with user consent.

How to disable cookies?

Users can change cookie settings in their browser at any time. Below are instructions for the most popular browsers:

  • Google Chrome — Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
  • Mozilla Firefox — Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
  • Safari — Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Microsoft Edge — Settings → Privacy → Cookies

Disabling cookies may affect the proper functioning of certain site features.

§8 Google Analytics

The gamak.eu website uses the Google Analytics 4 (GA4) service provided by Google Ireland Limited. GA4 uses cookies to analyze how users use the site.

Within GA4, we apply IP address anonymization, which means the user's IP address is truncated before being stored and analyzed. Data collected by GA4 is anonymous and does not allow direct identification of the user.

Users can block data collection by Google Analytics by installing a browser add-on: Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
