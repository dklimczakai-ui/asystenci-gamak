Our Story

From ice rinks to comprehensive
sports infrastructure

Gamak Sp. z o.o. was founded in 2012 in Jastrzebie-Zdroj, Poland. Initially, we specialized in delivering seasonal ice rinks and ice resurfacers for municipalities across Poland.

Over the years, we expanded our portfolio to include sport surfaces, padel courts, rental skates and isothermal floor systems. Today, we are one of the few companies in Poland offering a full spectrum of turnkey sports infrastructure.

Over 15 years of experience and 100+ completed projects across Poland. We serve municipalities, sports clubs and private investors — from design to handover and long-term maintenance.

Our Offer

What We Do

Comprehensive sports infrastructure — from design, through manufacturing, to installation and maintenance. One company, zero problems.

Ice Rinks

Mobile seasonal ice rinks with real ice, permanent hockey rinks with IIHF certification. Recreational and hockey boards, complete turnkey installation.

Padel Courts

Premium turnkey padel courts. Hot-dip galvanized, 12mm glass, UDEM and TÜV certifications. MSiT funding support.

Sport Surfaces

Modular surface systems for every discipline — inline hockey, multisport, tennis, courts, playgrounds, roller rinks.

Ice Resurfacers

From compact Ice Dingo to full-size Red Tiger. Seasonal maintenance, operator training, spare parts.

Rental Skates

Professional skates for rental operations. V-Lock Buckles system, replaceable insoles, hardened steel. 3-year warranty.

Ice Covertan

Isothermal floor — convert your ice rink in 4-6 hours without defrosting. Concerts, trade fairs, tournaments on ice.
