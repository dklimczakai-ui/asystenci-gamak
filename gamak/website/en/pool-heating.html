About the System

A surface that heats pool water

The GAMAK pool water heating system is an outdoor surface that serves two functions simultaneously — it is a comfortable poolside surface and a solar heat collector that heats the water. The surface is made from a poured mixture of EPDM granulate and EPDM mats.

It absorbs solar energy and transfers it to the pool's heating system, extending the swimming season and reducing water heating costs.

How It Works

How does the water-heating surface work?

EPDM (ethylene propylene diene monomer) surface has a natural ability to absorb solar radiation. Heat absorbed by the surface is transferred to a system of pipes installed beneath it, through which pool water or an intermediate medium flows.

Solar Absorption

The EPDM surface absorbs solar radiation and converts it into heat — functioning like a solar mat collector.

Pipes Beneath the Surface

A pipe system is installed beneath the EPDM layer, through which pool water or an intermediate medium flows.

Heated Water Returns to the Pool

The heated medium returns to the pool, raising the water temperature without additional electricity or gas consumption.

The operating principle is similar to solar mat collectors — with the difference that the collector is also a functional poolside surface.

Materials

What is the surface made of?

The surface consists of two components — it is poured wet and cured on-site, which allows it to be adapted to any shape and dimensions of the pool area.

EPDM Granulate

Fine particles of synthetic rubber bonded with resin. They form the top layer with controlled porosity and anti-slip texture.

EPDM Mats

A base layer providing resilience, shock absorption, and thermal insulation from the substrate.

Colors are selected individually — according to the investor's preferences. Darker variants absorb more solar energy, which increases heating efficiency.

Properties

EPDM poolside surface properties

Resilience and Shock Absorption

The surface is soft underfoot, reducing the risk of injury from falls on wet surfaces. Particularly important in public facilities with children.

Anti-Slip Properties

The EPDM granulate texture provides grip even on wet surfaces.

Solar Heat Absorption

Darker color variants absorb more energy, increasing the heating efficiency.

UV Resistance

EPDM does not degrade under prolonged sun exposure, which is crucial for outdoor surfaces.

Water and Pool Chemical Resistance

The surface does not absorb water and is resistant to chlorine and other disinfectants.

Custom Colors

Colors matched to the facility design — full surface personalization.

Benefits

Benefits of the pool water heating system

Extended Swimming Season

The heat-absorbing surface raises pool water temperature, allowing the season to start earlier in spring and end later in autumn — without increasing energy bills.

Lower Heating Costs

Solar energy absorbed by the surface is free. The system works as a supplement or alternative to traditional heaters (heat pumps, gas boilers, electric heaters).

Dual-Function Surface

The investor does not pay separately for a poolside surface and a solar collector — one solution serves both functions.

User Comfort and Safety

The resilient EPDM surface is comfortable under bare feet, anti-slip, and cushions falls.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the EPDM surface heat pool water?

The surface absorbs solar radiation and transfers heat to a system of pipes installed beneath it. Pool water (or an intermediate medium) flows through these pipes, heats up, and returns to the pool at a higher temperature.

By how many degrees does the surface raise the water temperature?

Efficiency depends on the surface area, sun exposure, color, and ambient temperature. Exact parameters are determined individually during the design phase.

Is the surface slippery around the pool?

No. EPDM granulate has a natural anti-slip texture that provides grip even on wet surfaces.

Is the surface resistant to chlorine and pool chemicals?

Yes. EPDM is resistant to chlorine, ozone, and other chemicals used in pool water disinfection.

Can the surface color be customized?

Yes. Colors are selected individually. Darker colors absorb more heat, which increases the heating efficiency.

Does the system work with an existing pool?

Yes. The surface can be installed around an existing pool and connected to the current heating system as an additional heat source.
