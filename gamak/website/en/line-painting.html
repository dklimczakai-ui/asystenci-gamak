About the Service

Sport line marking and painting — from ice rinks to multi-purpose fields

GAMAK provides professional line painting on hockey rinks, sports fields, and modular surfaces (polypropylene, EPDM). Markings are applied with millimeter precision, in compliance with sports federation regulations.

We paint a standard hockey rink in 8 hours. The facility returns to service the same day. Minimal downtime, maximum precision.

The service covers the full scope of work — from consultation and marking design, through measurements and painting, to quality control and handover. We operate throughout Poland.

Scope of Services

What we paint

Hockey Rinks

Center lines, goal lines, defensive zones. Face-off circles and dots. Markings compliant with IIHF and PZHL regulations. Application of sponsor logos and graphics under the ice surface.

Sports Fields

Multi-purpose markings — football, basketball, volleyball, tennis, handball, badminton. Each sport in a different color. Paints resistant to intensive use, UV, and weather conditions.

2in1 and 3in1 Facilities

Markings on polypropylene and EPDM surfaces in facilities combining a sports field with an ice rink. Materials adapted to surfaces that endure conditions from frost to heat.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to paint lines on a hockey rink?

We paint a standard hockey rink in 8 hours. The facility returns to service the same day.

What sports can be marked on a single field?

Multiple sports can be marked on a single surface — football, basketball, volleyball, tennis, handball, badminton. Each sport is painted in a different color for clear readability.

Can sponsor logos be placed under the ice?

Yes. We apply sponsor logos, club crests, and custom graphics under the ice surface. They are durable, legible, and professional.

Does GAMAK paint lines on modular surfaces (polypropylene, EPDM)?

Yes. We apply markings on polypropylene and EPDM surfaces — in 2in1, 3in1 facilities and multi-purpose sports fields. We use materials adapted to the specific characteristics of these surfaces.

What area does GAMAK serve?

We operate throughout Poland and Europe. Experience from over 125 projects.

Is the line painting compliant with IIHF regulations?

Yes. Markings on hockey rinks are applied in compliance with IIHF (International Ice Hockey Federation) and PZHL (Polish Ice Hockey Association) regulations.
