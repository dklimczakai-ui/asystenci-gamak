Engineered for European Climate

Built to withstand
every harsh winter.

SPHC Steel 100×100×3mm

Main posts 100×100×3mm, load-bearing beams 100×50×3mm. The 3mm wall thickness eliminates vibrations during strong winds and protects glass from cracking — a standard required in European wind zones.

Post-Weld Hot-Dip Galvanizing — 3 Layers

The finished welded structure is immersed in zinc at 450°C — only after welding. Zinc penetrates the welds and inner profiles. Then powder coating is applied. Result: C5 class, 25+ years without corrosion in European climate.

UDEM Certifications × 4 + TÜV Rheinland

4 UDEM certifications for: steel (EN 10219), glass (EN 12150-1+2), surface (EN 14877) and LED (EN 60598). Glass confirmed by TÜV Rheinland. Documentation ready for public tenders — the Supervising Inspector will not reject the bid.

LED Lighting 8×200W · 300 Lux · 6500K

8 lights symmetrically positioned — zero shadows during smashes. 6500K color temperature ideal for video broadcasting. In the Full Panoramic model, lighting masts are integrated into the post structure.

Court Lines Woven into the Turf

White lines are embossed during turf production — not glued on site. Result: geometrically perfect lines, durable throughout the entire surface lifespan.

Glass Connectors 5mm · 304 Stainless Steel

18 panels of 12mm glass are held by 304 stainless steel connectors with 5mm thickness. 304 stainless steel does not rust for decades outdoors — the glass remains safe throughout the entire court lifespan.

Machine-Welded Mesh 1:1

Every wire intersection is machine-welded — not by hand, not selectively. Smooth, polished surface with no sharp edges. Wire 4mm, mesh 50×50mm, hot-dip galvanized Q235B.
