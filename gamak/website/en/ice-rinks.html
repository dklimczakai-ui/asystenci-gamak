What We Do

Recreational Ice Rinks — A Seasonal Attraction for Your City

Recreational ice rinks are seasonal sports facilities designed for outdoor installation — in city squares, parks, sports and school complexes. GAMAK delivers complete mobile ice rinks with recreational dasher boards — from design through installation to service and operator training.

We build facilities with surface areas from 200 to over 800 m2, tailored to the needs of municipalities, MOSiR sports centers, and private investors.

Ice Rink Components

What Does a Recreational Ice Rink Include?

Every GAMAK mobile ice rink consists of five components. This setup allows the rink to be operational within days and dismantled after the season without permanent alteration to the surface.

Modular Refrigeration System

Cooling pipes in various technologies, connected in sections adapted to the shape and size of the ice surface.

Protective Dasher Boards (Recreational)

Lightweight aluminum profile structure with HDPE panels, protecting skaters and separating the ice surface from the surroundings.

Chiller Unit

Compact unit producing coolant with parameters from -8C/-11C to -9C/-12C, depending on the rink size.

Glycol Installation

Tanks, circulation pump, and glycol as the coolant carrier in a closed loop system.

Subgrade Thermal Insulation

A layer protecting the ground from freezing and ensuring ice surface stability.

Technical Specifications

Technical Parameters — Example of a 600-800 m2 Ice Rink

A recreational ice rink with a surface area of 600-800 m2 is one of the most popular formats chosen by towns and cities.

Ice Surface Area 600-800 m2
Simultaneous Capacity 80-100 skaters
Daily Operating Time up to 14 hours
Operating Temperature Range up to +10C / +15C (at low wind speed)
Cooling Parameters -8C/-11C or -9C/-12C
Installation Type Seasonal, demountable

The ice rink operates throughout the entire winter season — from November to March — and can function even at above-zero ambient temperatures, provided that wind conditions are moderate.

Recreational Dasher Boards

Recreational Dasher Boards — Construction and Dimensions

GAMAK recreational dasher boards are a lightweight alternative to heavy steel fencing. The structure is based on aluminum profiles filled with white HDPE (high-density polyethylene) panels.

The aluminum construction is lighter than steel, corrosion-resistant, and suitable for both mobile (seasonal) and permanent (year-round) installations.

Handrail

Top edge in blue or red, serving as a grip for skaters.

Kickplate

Lower strip 20 cm high in yellow, protecting the bottom edge from mechanical damage.

Modules

Standard width of 2 m, height of 1.0-1.2 m. Modular connection system — easy assembly and disassembly.

Corner Curves

Radius of 4-5 m, providing smooth transitions at rink corners.

Entry Gates

Width of 0.9 m. Additional service gate for ice resurfacer access.

Target Audience

Who Are Recreational Ice Rinks For?

Mobile ice rinks with recreational dasher boards are most commonly ordered by municipalities, MOSiR sports centers, resorts, and event organizers.

City and Municipal Offices

A seasonal attraction for residents, funded from own budgets or grants (MSiT, Polski Lad, EU funds).

MOSiR and Sports Centers

Supplementing the sports offering during the winter season. An ice rink as a regular feature on the center's calendar.

Resorts and Hotels

An additional tourist attraction that increases interest in the facility during the winter season.

Shopping Centers and Event Organizers

Temporary promotional installation for Christmas markets, festivals, and outdoor events.

Municipalities with 5,000-100,000 residents represent the largest customer group. A recreational ice rink with a surface area of 600-800 m2 fits within the investment budgets of most municipalities — especially with co-financing from government programs.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a recreational ice rink cost?

The price depends on the ice surface area, dasher board configuration, and additional equipment. Ice rinks delivered by GAMAK range from 300,000 to 2 million PLN net. We prepare a detailed quote after determining the specifications.

Does a recreational ice rink operate at above-zero temperatures?

Yes. The refrigeration system maintains the ice surface at ambient temperatures up to +10C, and in favorable conditions (low humidity, light wind) up to +15C.

How long does it take to install a mobile ice rink?

Installation of an ice rink with a surface area of 600-800 m2 takes from several days to two weeks, depending on terrain conditions and site preparation.

Does GAMAK assist with funding applications?

Yes. We provide free support to municipalities in preparing technical documentation for grant applications from MSiT programs, Polski Lad, and EU funds.

Can recreational dasher boards be reused in the next season?

Yes. The modular aluminum structure with HDPE panels is designed for multiple assembly and disassembly cycles without loss of performance.
