Compact Models

Compact Resurfacers for Smaller Ice Sheets

Perfect for recreational, mobile, and seasonal ice rinks. Agile, electric, easy to operate.

IceDingo

Specialized ice edging machine. Compact, electric, indispensable along boards and corners — where a full-size resurfacer cannot reach.

  • 360 mm blade — for ice edging
  • Fully electric drive
  • Perfect for working along boards
  • Compact dimensions, easy transport

IceFox

Compact and fast resurfacer for ice sheets up to 800 m2. Electric drive, agile design — perfect for small recreational and seasonal rinks.

  • 1400 mm blade
  • Ice surface up to 800 m2
  • Fully electric drive
  • Compact — fast and agile
  • PLC control with touchscreen

IcePuma

Easy-to-operate resurfacer with lever controls. Works well on ice sheets up to 1,200 m2 — an easy start for operators with no experience.

  • 1700 mm blade
  • Ice surface up to 1,200 m2
  • Fully electric drive
  • Lever controls — simple operation
  • SmartConnect — remote diagnostics

IceWolf Series

Mid-Range — IceWolf Series

A proven series of resurfacers for mid-size ice sheets. Three variants — Smart, Classic, and Pro — tailored to different needs and budgets.

IceWolf Smart

Proven design in a modern edition. Electric resurfacer for ice sheets up to 1,860 m2 with optional Lithio lithium battery. Reliable and cost-effective.

  • 2000 / 2150 / 2300 mm blade
  • Ice surface up to 1,860 m2
  • Electric drive (Lithio option)
  • Touchscreen and PLC control
  • SmartConnect — remote diagnostics

IceWolf Classic

Reliable construction proven at hundreds of ice rinks. Electric drive with Lithio option. Solid, breakdown-free, easy to maintain.

  • 2000 / 2150 / 2300 mm blade
  • Ice surface up to 1,800 m2
  • Electric drive (Lithio option)
  • Proven, reliable construction
  • Quick blade replacement

IceWolf Pro

Professional version with joystick for precision control. Electric with Lithio option — maximum operator comfort for daily use.

  • 2000 / 2150 / 2300 mm blade
  • Ice surface up to 1,800 m2
  • Electric drive (Lithio option)
  • Joystick control
  • Touchscreen and SmartConnect

IcePanther LITHIO

The new generation of Engo resurfacers. Fully electric with lithium battery, optional LED lighting. Efficient on ice sheets up to 2,500 m2.

IceTiger

Full power on large ice sheets. Resurfacer with joystick for precision control, electric with Lithio option. Handles ice sheets up to 3,000 m2.

  • Joystick control
