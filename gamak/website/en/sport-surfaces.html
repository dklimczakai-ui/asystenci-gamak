About the offer

Two Brands — Full Range of Sport Surfaces

GAMAK supplies modular sport surfaces from two brands — Czech Stilmat and Polish NS Pro. We deliver multisport courts, tennis courts, roller rinks, inline hockey surfaces and playgrounds.

We install surfaces outdoors and indoors — on new substrate, existing asphalt or as an overlay on old polyurethane surfacing. GAMAK is the authorized Stilmat distributor in Poland and the manufacturer of the NS Pro brand.

Installation

Three Ways to Build a Court

Stilmat and NS Pro surfaces can be installed on three types of substrate — depending on the condition of the existing facility and budget.

1. Asphalt Court Renovation

Surface installed directly on existing asphalt, after filling gaps, sealing cracks, milling bumps and leveling. An old cracked court gets new life without demolition costs.

2. Overlay on Old Polyurethane Surface

Instead of costly demolition and disposal of the old surface, we apply a stone aggregate and resin overlay that creates a stable base for the modular surface. Saves time and money.

3. Court Built from Scratch

For undeveloped sites — a court on a specially prepared, compacted subbase. A cheaper and faster solution than a concrete slab or paving. Feasible even on challenging terrain.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between Stilmat and NS Pro?

Stilmat is a Czech brand with over 30 years of experience, holding World Skate (inline hockey) and ITF (tennis) certificates — used in international competitions. NS Pro is GAMAK's own brand — designed and delivered directly by us, with full quality control and a wide range of applications: courts, playgrounds, roller rinks, racquet courts.

Can modular surfacing be installed on old asphalt?

Yes. After filling gaps and leveling the surface, Stilmat or NS Pro surfacing is installed directly on the existing asphalt. There is no need to demolish the old court.

Can the surface withstand harsh winters?

Yes. Stilmat and NS Pro polypropylene surfaces are resistant to frost, rain, UV and temperature fluctuations. They do not crack or deform. They can remain outdoors all year round.

How long does court installation take?

Modular surface installation takes from a few days to two weeks — depending on the area and substrate condition. Installation requires no wet construction works.

Which sports can be played on a single court?

Multisport surfaces allow basketball, volleyball, handball, futsal, tennis, badminton, floorball, as well as roller skating and inline hockey. Lines for individual sports are marked in different colors.

Does GAMAK assist with funding?

Yes. We support municipalities in preparing technical documentation for grant applications from MSiT programmes, Polish Deal and EU funds.

Is the surface safe for children?

Yes. Playground surfaces (NS Pro, EPDM) meet the EN 1177 standard — they cushion falls and protect against injuries. Sport surfaces feature an anti-slip texture and micro cushioning.
