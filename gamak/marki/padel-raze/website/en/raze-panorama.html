Why Bestseller

One court.
Zero compromises.

RAZE PANORAMA is the flagship model of the PADEL RAZE line. Full 360° glazing around the entire court eliminates visibility blind spots — spectators can watch the game from every side, and cameras capture the action without obstruction. This is a court built for tournaments, broadcasts and facilities that are meant to be a showcase.

What sets PANORAMA apart from the competition is the integrated lighting masts. In standard courts, lighting is mounted on separate poles around the playing area — they take up space, disrupt aesthetics and create shadows. In RAZE PANORAMA, 6-meter LED masts are built directly into the 100×100×3mm corner columns. Eight 200W LED lights provide uniform 300 Lux across the entire playing surface, and the 6500K color temperature is optimized for video broadcasts.

The structure meets European standards confirmed by four UDEM certificates: steel (EN 10219), glass (EN 12150-1+2), turf (EN 14877) and LED lighting (EN 60598). The 12mm tempered glass additionally holds a TÜV Rheinland certificate. For municipalities, this means procurement-ready documentation that a building inspector will accept without objections.

The entire steel structure is hot-dip galvanized after welding — zinc penetrates the welds and profile interiors, providing C5 corrosion class and 25+ years of durability in European climate. Robotic welding ensures repeatable precision and wind resistance at level 10+ (160–180 mph). 304 stainless steel connectors with precision CNC cutting complete a system where every component works in harmony.

RAZE PANORAMA Technology

3 pillars of engineering.

Steel structure

Corner columns 100×100×3mm Q235B with integrated 6m LED masts. Load-bearing beams 100×50×3mm, mesh frame 50×30×2.5mm. Entire structure robotically welded and hot-dip galvanized after welding — zinc penetrates welds and profile interiors.

  • C5 corrosion class — 3-layer protection
  • Robotic welding — repeatable precision
  • Integrated LED masts — no separate poles
  • Wind resistance: level 10+ (160–180 mph)
  • 304 Stainless Steel connectors, CNC cut

360° glass system

18 panels of 12mm tempered glass measuring 2×3m. G1/G2 explosion-proof glass with 8 chamfered holes and polished corners. Full glazing around the court eliminates blind spots and provides uninterrupted visibility.

  • EN 12150-1 — European tempered glass standard
  • TÜV Rheinland — independent certification
  • CE, SGCC, IGCC, ISO, UDEM
  • G1/G2 explosion-proof — player safety
  • Polished corners — eliminates cut risk

Premium turf surface

Monofilament synthetic turf made of 100% reinforced PE, 12mm pile height, Dtex 10000, density 58,800 fibers/m². Three-layer PP+PP+PU backing ensures dimensional stability and deformation resistance. Roll size 4000×2000mm, total 209m² per court.

  • UV resistance: SGS UVA 5000h
  • Water permeability: ≥30L/min/m²
  • Gauge 3/16 — precision tufting
  • Court lines woven during production
  • EN 14877 — UDEM turf certificate

Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ — RAZE PANORAMA

How does the RAZE PANORAMA differ from the RAZE PRO?
RAZE PANORAMA is a Full Panoramic 360° construction — full glazing around the entire court provides uninterrupted visibility from every side. The key difference: LED lighting masts are integrated into the corner columns (no separate poles), resulting in cleaner aesthetics and eliminating visual obstacles. RAZE PRO has separate lighting masts and a standard panoramic layout. Both models share identical turf density parameters (Dtex 10000) and 304 SS CNC connectors.
Is the PANORAMA court suitable for professional tournaments?
Yes. RAZE PANORAMA meets the requirements for professional padel competitions. Full 360° glazing enables TV broadcasts without blind spots. The 300 Lux lighting at 6500K color temperature is optimized for cameras. Court dimensions of 20×10m comply with international standards. The structure holds UDEM ×4 and TÜV Rheinland certificates.
How do the integrated LED masts work?
In the RAZE PANORAMA model, 6m lighting masts are built directly into the 100×100×3mm corner columns. This eliminates the need for separate lighting poles around the court — clean aesthetic lines and no visual obstacles. Eight 200W LED lights (IP65) provide uniform 300 Lux across the entire playing surface. The 6500K color temperature is ideal for video broadcasts and comfortable evening play.
What is the wind resistance?
RAZE PANORAMA withstands winds of Beaufort scale level 10+ (160–180 mph). This is achieved through 100×100×3mm Q235B corner columns, 100×50×3mm load-bearing beams, robotic welding and a reinforced concrete foundation (15cm concrete + 1.2cm steel rebar every 25cm). The structure is designed in accordance with Eurocodes (EN 1991) for European wind and snow zones.
Can the colors be customized?
Yes. The turf is available in three colors: green, blue and red. Structural columns come in black as standard, with the option of powder coating in any custom RAL color on request. This allows the court to match the visual identity of a municipality, club or sponsor.
How long does delivery take from order to handover?
Complete delivery takes 10–16 weeks. Production of the structure and components takes 8–10 days. Full timeline: building design (2–4 weeks), production and shipping (6–10 weeks), on-site installation and technical handover (1–2 weeks). When ordered by March, the court is ready for the summer season.
