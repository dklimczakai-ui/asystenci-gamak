About the model

A smart entry
into professional padel.

RAZE CLASSIC is the model that proves a professional padel court doesn't have to cost a fortune. Designed for municipalities building their first court, clubs starting their padel journey, and investors looking for the best quality-to-price ratio.

The quality foundations are identical to the premium models. Q235B steel — the same grade as in RAZE PRO and RAZE PANORAMA. 12mm tempered glass with UDEM EN 12150-1 certificate — the exact same G1/G2 explosion-proof panels, with 8 countersunk holes and polished corners. Hot-dip galvanized after welding in three layers — C5 corrosion class, which means 25+ years without corrosion in European climate.

Corner columns 80x80x3mm are a proven profile for standard installations. 12 reinforced glass columns 100x50x3mm ensure the stability of the entire structure. Robotic welding guarantees repeatable precision of every joint. All connectors are made of 304 stainless steel — bolts, joints, glass mountings.

The 10mm monofilament turf with a Dtex 9000 parameter and a density of 46,200 stitches per square meter is a durable surface proven in hundreds of installations. The PU backing at 700 g/m² and water permeability above 30 L/min/m² guarantee rapid drying after rain.

RAZE CLASSIC is the most frequently chosen model by municipalities entering the padel market. Full procurement documentation with 4 UDEM certificates is ready to submit. The building inspector will not reject the bid — every component has confirmed compliance with European standards.

RAZE CLASSIC Technology

3 pillars of quality.

Q235B Steel with C5 galvanization

Corner columns 80x80x3mm in Q235B grade — high-strength structural steel. 12 reinforced glass columns 100x50x3mm stabilize the glass panels around the entire perimeter.

Load-bearing beams 80x40x3mm (upper and lower) ensure structural rigidity. Mesh frame 40x20x2mm precisely holds the mesh panels.

Robotic welding — every joint identical. After welding, the entire structure is submerged in zinc at 450°C. Zinc penetrates welds and profile interiors. Result: C5 class, 25+ years without corrosion.

80x80x3mm Q235B C5 Robot-weld

12mm tempered glass

Identical glass to all PADEL RAZE models. Panels 2995x1995mm, thickness 12mm — 18 panels per court. G1/G2 explosion-proof type with polished corners.

8 precision CNC countersunk holes in each panel. Mounted on 5mm thick 304 stainless steel connectors.

Certificates: EN 12150-1 (tempered glass), CE, TÜV Rheinland, UDEM. Full documentation ready for procurement.

12mm 18 panels EN 12150-1 TÜV

10mm monofilament turf

Monofilament turf with 10mm pile height and Dtex 9000 parameter. Density of 46,200 stitches per square meter. 100% polyester (PE) — fiber composition resistant to UV and abrasion.

Stitch rate: 22 stitches per 10 cm. PU backing at ~700 g/m² ensures dimensional stability and durability.

Water permeability above 30 L/min/m² — the court is ready for play within minutes after rain. Court lines woven into the turf during production.

10mm Dtex 9000 PU 700g/m² 100% PE

Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ — RAZE CLASSIC

Does the RAZE CLASSIC meet public procurement standards?
Yes. RAZE CLASSIC holds 4 UDEM certificates: steel (EN 10219), glass (EN 12150-1+2), turf (EN 14877), LED lighting (EN 60598). The glass is additionally certified by TÜV Rheinland. The structure meets C5 corrosion class. Documentation is ready for procurement tenders — it meets the requirements of tender specifications and building inspectors.
How does the RAZE CLASSIC differ from the RAZE PRO?
Main differences: corner columns 80x80x3mm (Classic) vs 100x100x3mm (Pro), turf 10mm Dtex 9000 (Classic) vs 13mm Dtex 10000 (Pro), turf density 46,200/m² (Classic) vs 58,800/m² (Pro). Both versions have identical C5 corrosion class, the same 12mm tempered glass with UDEM certificate and the same LED 8x200W IP65 lighting. RAZE CLASSIC is a solid base at a lower price — ideal to start with.
Is 10mm turf sufficient?
Yes. The 10mm monofilament turf with Dtex 9000 and a density of 46,200 stitches/m² is a proven surface for recreational and amateur play. The PU 700 g/m² backing ensures durability, and water permeability above 30 L/min/m² guarantees rapid drainage after rain. For intensive tournament use, we recommend RAZE PRO with 13mm Dtex 10000 turf.
Can I upgrade the court to a higher model later?
The synthetic turf surface is replaceable — it can be upgraded to a higher class (e.g., 13mm Dtex 10000) without modifying the steel structure. The RAZE CLASSIC steel structure is designed for 25+ years of durability and does not require upgrading. Replacing the turf is the only change that can be easily made in the future.
What is the warranty on the RAZE CLASSIC?
RAZE CLASSIC comes with a 2-year warranty on the steel structure, glass and turf surface. A service contract with periodic inspections is optionally available. The structure with C5 hot-dip galvanization is designed for 25+ years of operation in European climate without corrosion.
How long does RAZE CLASSIC delivery take from order to handover?
Complete delivery takes 10–16 weeks: building design (2–4 weeks), production and shipping (6–10 weeks, including 8–10 days of production), installation and handover (1–2 weeks). When ordered by March, the court is ready for the summer season.
