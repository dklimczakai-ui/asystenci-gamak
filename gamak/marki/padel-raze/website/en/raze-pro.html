About the Model

The strongest frame
in the PADEL RAZE range.

RAZE PRO is a padel court designed for professional use. Compared to the RAZE CLASSIC model, the PRO features corner columns with a 100×100×3mm cross-section — 25% larger than the standard 80×80mm. Thicker load-bearing beams 100×50×3mm and a mesh frame 50×30×2mm create a structure prepared for level 10+ winds and years of outdoor use.

The 13mm turf surface is the highest in the entire PADEL RAZE line. Monofilament PE anti-UV fibers with a density of 58,800 fibers/m² and a Dtex 10000 parameter ensure predictable ball bounce and better joint cushioning for players. The fleeced backing with PET reinforcement and an 800 g/m² polyurethane coating guarantees dimensional stability even at extreme temperatures — from -25°C to +60°C. 2mm perforation provides water permeability of at least 30L/min/m², eliminating puddles after rain.

12mm tempered glass with precise 2995×1995mm dimensions, 8 countersunk holes and polished corners. G1/G2 explosion-proof type, certified EN 12150-1, TÜV Rheinland and UDEM. 18 panels per court. The entire steel structure is hot-dip galvanized after welding — zinc at 450°C penetrates the welds and profile interiors. Then powder coating is applied. Result: C5 corrosion class and 25+ years of durability in European climate.

RAZE PRO is the choice for padel clubs with intensive utilization, outdoor sports centers, hotels and resorts with a premium offering, and commercial installations where the court operates 8+ hours daily year-round.

Technology

Reinforced steel frame

Columns 100×100×3mm Q235B

25% larger cross-section than the CLASSIC model. 3mm wall thickness eliminates vibrations in gusty winds. Q235B steel — a structural grade with guaranteed mechanical properties.

Hot-dip galvanized after welding — C5 class

The finished welded structure is submerged in zinc at 450°C. Zinc penetrates the welds and profile interiors. 3-layer protection: zinc + primer + powder coating. Result: 25+ years without corrosion in European climate.

Robotic welding

All joints are automatically welded — repeatable quality, uniform welds, zero human error. Structure prepared for wind loads of level 10+ for outdoor installations.

50×50×4mm machine-welded mesh

1×2m sections, 4mm wire, 50×50mm aperture. Shot-blasted + galvanized. 0% water absorption. Smooth surface without sharp edges — safe on player contact.

Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ — RAZE PRO

How does the RAZE PRO differ from the RAZE CLASSIC?
RAZE PRO has reinforced corner columns 100×100×3mm (vs 80×80×3mm in CLASSIC), thicker load-bearing beams 100×50×3mm (vs 80×40×3mm), a higher turf surface 13mm Dtex 10000 with a density of 58,800/m² (vs 10mm Dtex 9000, 46,200/m² in CLASSIC) and a larger mesh frame 50×30×2mm (vs 40×20×2mm). RAZE PRO is designed for intensive commercial use and outdoor installations exposed to strong winds.
Why is 13mm turf better?
The 13mm turf is the highest in the PADEL RAZE range. Higher fibers provide better joint cushioning and more predictable ball bounce. The density of 58,800 fibers/m² and Dtex 10000 ensure greater durability under intensive use (8+ hours daily). The fleeced + PET backing with 800 g/m² PU coating guarantees dimensional stability even at extreme temperatures.
Is the RAZE PRO suitable for outdoor use?
Yes, RAZE PRO is designed for outdoor installations. The reinforced 100×100×3mm structure withstands winds of level 10+. Hot-dip galvanized C5 protects the steel for 25+ years in European climate. The PE turf with anti-UV fibers and 2mm perforation provides water permeability of at least 30L/min/m². G1/G2 explosion-proof glass is resistant to impacts and weather conditions.
What certificates does the RAZE PRO hold?
RAZE PRO holds 4 UDEM certificates: steel (EN 10219), glass (EN 12150-1+2), turf (EN 14877), LED lighting (EN 60598). The glass is additionally certified by TÜV Rheinland and holds CE, SGCC, IGCC, and ISO certificates. All documentation is ready for public procurement tenders.
Can I order the RAZE PRO court with integrated lighting?
RAZE PRO comes standard with separate lighting masts 100×50×3mm with 8×200W LED IP65 lamps. If you need lighting integrated into the structural columns, we recommend the RAZE PANORAMA model, which has LED masts built into the 100×100×3mm columns. Contact us for a custom configuration.
