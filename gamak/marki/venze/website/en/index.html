FAQ

How much do VENZE rental ice skates cost?
Pricing depends on the model and order volume. V-RENTAL PRO and V-RENTAL KIDS are available in packages starting from 50 pairs. VENZE offers prices 64–95% lower than European competitors at comparable quality. Contact us for an individual B2B quote.
What is the lifespan of VENZE rental ice skates?
The V-RENTAL PRO shell withstands a minimum of 3 seasons of intensive use at 12 hours per day. 3-year warranty on the shell. Replaceable DRY-TECH liners and STEEL-HD blades extend lifespan without replacing the entire skate.
Does VENZE deliver to ice rinks across Europe?
Yes. We deliver throughout Poland and Central Europe. Delivery time: 6–10 weeks from order. We serve private ice rinks, municipal sports facilities, and local government institutions.
What are V-PENGUIN skating aids?
V-PENGUIN are professional skating aids for learning to skate on ice. Made from durable HD polymer with anti-slip handles and adjustable height of 70–100 cm. Ideal for children and adult beginners. They increase ice rink revenue by 15–25%.
What about service and spare parts?
We offer full service: replaceable DRY-TECH liners, STEEL-HD blades, V-LOCK buckles — all available as spare parts. Thanks to modular design, repairs are fast and affordable. Service cost 80% lower than competitors.
