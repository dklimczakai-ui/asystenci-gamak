Child safety

A surface that protects against falls

A playground is where children learn about the world through movement. NS Pro is a surface that lets them run, jump and play freely — without the risk of injury from falls onto a hard surface. Elastic polypropylene absorbs impacts, the non-slip structure prevents slipping, and rounded edges protect against abrasions.

The material is fully non-toxic — no phthalates, no BPA, no heavy metals. It complies with European safety standards for children's facilities. 1-day installation, no puddles thanks to built-in drainage, maintenance-free upkeep. Chosen by kindergartens, schools, municipalities and residential estates across Central Europe.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is the surface safe for small children?+
Yes. NS Pro surfaces are made of non-toxic polypropylene, safe for children of any age. The elastic construction absorbs falls, the non-slip surface minimises the risk of slipping, and rounded edges eliminate the risk of abrasions.
Does the surface meet safety standards?+
Yes. NS Pro surfaces comply with European playground safety standards. Polypropylene is non-toxic and contains no phthalates or heavy metals. The structure provides fall absorption and non-slip properties in line with requirements for children's facilities.
How do I clean the playground surface?+
The NS Pro surface is practically maintenance-free. Regular sweeping and occasional water rinsing — preferably with a pressure washer — is enough. If needed, mild cleaning agents can be used. It requires no painting, renovation or seasonal maintenance.
Can the surface be used in a kindergarten?+
Yes. The NS Pro surface is one of the most popular solutions for kindergartens in Central Europe. It meets all safety requirements, is easy to maintain and attractive in colour. We provide full documentation required for inspection and approval.
From what height does it absorb falls?+
Depending on panel thickness, the NS Pro surface absorbs falls from heights up to 1.5 m. For facilities requiring greater absorption (slides, climbing frames) we offer panels with increased thickness. We will help choose the optimal solution for your specific playground.
