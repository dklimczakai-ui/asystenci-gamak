FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Can I install a garden court myself?+
Yes. NS Pro surfaces feature a click-lock snap system that allows DIY installation without glue or specialist tools. A backyard court can be installed in a few hours. All you need is an even, hardened sub-base — concrete, asphalt or paving stones.
Is the surface safe for children?+
Yes. NS Pro surfaces are made of non-toxic polypropylene that is safe for children of any age. The elastic material absorbs falls and protects joints. The non-slip surface minimises the risk of slipping even after rain.
What surface colours are available?+
We offer over 20 colours. You can combine colours, create playing zones, mark sports lines and design unique layouts. We will help choose colours that match your garden and surroundings.
What size of court should I choose?+
It depends on the discipline and available space. A 3×3m mini court is enough for hopscotch or individual practice. A 5×8m court works well for mini basketball or badminton. Full-size volleyball needs 9×18m. We will help you choose the optimal size.
Can the court be extended later?+
Yes. The NS Pro modular system lets you buy additional panels and extend the court at any time. You can start with a smaller facility and expand it as needed. Every panel is replaceable — a damaged one is swapped individually.
