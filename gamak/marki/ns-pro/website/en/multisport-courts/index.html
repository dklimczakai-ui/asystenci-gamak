FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What sports can be played on a multisport court?+
An NS Pro multisport court allows you to play basketball, volleyball, handball, tennis, badminton, floorball and many other disciplines. One surface supports different sports thanks to universal shock absorption and grip parameters. We mark sports lines in chosen colours for specific disciplines.
Is the surface weather resistant?+
Yes. NS Pro surfaces are 100% resistant to rain, frost down to -40°C, snow and UV radiation. The built-in drainage system removes water instantly after rainfall, and the polypropylene material does not crack, does not fade and requires no seasonal maintenance.
How long does installation of a multisport court take?+
A full-size multisport court usually takes 2-5 working days. Smaller facilities can be ready in 1-2 days. The click-lock snap system requires no glue or specialist tools. The court is ready to play immediately after installation.
Do you deliver public procurement contracts for municipalities?+
Yes. NS Pro regularly cooperates with local government units on public procurement projects. We provide full technical documentation, certificates and approvals required in tender procedures. We support municipalities in selecting surfaces and preparing specifications.
What sub-base is required for the court?+
The NS Pro surface is installed on a hardened, even sub-base — usually concrete, asphalt or paving stones. It does not require pouring a new slab. In many cases we can install it over an existing, worn-out court without dismantling the old surface.
