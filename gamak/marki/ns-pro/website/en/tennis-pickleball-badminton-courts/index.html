FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What certifications does the tennis court surface have?+
The tennis court surface has an ITF (International Tennis Federation) certificate in the Medium-Fast category. The pickleball surface meets USAPA (USA Pickleball Association) standards and the badminton surface meets BWF (Badminton World Federation) standards. The certifications confirm top-quality playing parameters.
Can the surface be installed on an existing court?+
Yes. The modular surface can be installed directly on an existing, worn-out hard court — concrete, asphalt or an old acrylic court. It does not require removal of the old surface. Court modernisation takes just 1-2 days, minimising downtime for the facility.
Is the surface suitable for tennis, pickleball and badminton?+
Yes. The surface is optimised for racquet sports — it delivers the right parameters for tennis (predictable ball bounce), pickleball (control at low bounce) and badminton (appropriate shuttle dynamics). Lines for different disciplines can be marked on a single court.
Does the surface require seasonal maintenance?+
No. This is one of the biggest advantages over clay. The NS Pro surface does not require watering, rolling, repainting lines or seasonal renovation. Sweeping and occasional water rinsing is enough. Thousands in savings each year on court upkeep.
How soon can you play after rain?+
An NS Pro court is ready to play almost immediately after rainfall. The built-in drainage system removes water from the surface in a few minutes. This is a huge advantage over clay, which requires hours of drying and rolling after rain.
