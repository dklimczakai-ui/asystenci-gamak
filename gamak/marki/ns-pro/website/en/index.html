About us

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How long does installation of an NS Pro sports surface take? +
Installation time depends on the size of the facility. A backyard court can be installed DIY in a few hours thanks to the click-lock snap system. A tennis court is modernised in 1-2 days. A full-size multisport court usually takes 2-5 working days. Installation requires no glue or specialist tools.
What material are the surfaces made of? +
NS Pro surfaces are made of top-quality polypropylene (PP). The material is resistant to UV radiation, frost down to -40°C, rain and intensive use. It is fully non-toxic, eco-friendly and 100% recyclable.
Are the surfaces suitable for outdoor use year-round? +
Yes. NS Pro surfaces are designed for outdoor use in all weather conditions — rain, frost, snow and UV radiation. The built-in drainage system removes water immediately after rainfall. The material does not crack, does not fade and retains elasticity even in low temperatures.
What certifications do NS Pro surfaces have? +
Tennis court surfaces are ITF certified (Medium-Fast category). Pickleball surfaces meet USAPA standards, and badminton surfaces meet BWF standards. All products comply with European safety norms and we provide full documentation for public procurement.
What is the cost of maintaining a sports surface? +
NS Pro surfaces are practically maintenance-free. They require no painting, watering, rolling or costly renovations. Regular sweeping and rinsing with water is the only maintenance required. Damaged panels can be replaced individually, without replacing the entire surface.
Do you deliver public procurement contracts for municipalities? +
Yes. NS Pro regularly cooperates with local government units on public procurement projects. We provide full technical documentation, certificates and approvals required in tender procedures. We support municipalities in selecting surfaces and preparing technical specifications.
