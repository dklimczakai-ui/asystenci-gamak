FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is the surface suitable for inline hockey?+
Yes. The surface is designed for both recreational roller skating and inline hockey. Hardened polypropylene is resistant to wheel abrasion, impacts from hockey sticks and pucks. The seamless construction provides a perfectly smooth surface for dynamic play.
Can the rink be quickly dismantled after an event?+
Yes. The modular system allows fast installation and disassembly of the surface — an ideal solution for temporary venues, sports events and city festivities. Panels can be assembled and disassembled many times without losing quality or technical parameters.
How does the surface handle rain?+
The surface is 100% weather resistant. The built-in drainage system removes rainwater so the rink is ready for use almost immediately after rainfall. The material does not absorb water and does not change properties in wet conditions.
What sub-base is required for the rink?+
The NS Pro surface can be installed on a hardened, even sub-base — concrete, asphalt, paving stones or another hard surface. For mobile roller rinks a levelled slab is enough. We will help assess the existing sub-base and prepare specifications for the investment.
How long does the surface last under intensive use?+
The NS Pro surface was designed for intensive, professional use. It withstands years of exploitation by inline hockey clubs, city roller rinks and commercial venues. Damaged panels can be replaced individually without replacing the entire surface.
