FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Can the surface be installed over existing parquet?+
Yes. NS Pro indoor surface systems can be installed directly over existing, worn-out parquet or other hard flooring. There is no need to dismantle the old surface — which significantly reduces time, lowers costs and eliminates dust and noise.
How long does modernising a gymnasium take?+
Modernising a gymnasium with NS Pro systems usually takes 1-2 working days. In many cases the refurbishment can be carried out over a weekend, minimising downtime — on Friday after lessons the old elements are removed, and on Sunday the hall is ready.
Which sports can be played on the indoor surface?+
NS Pro indoor surfaces are versatile and suitable for basketball, volleyball, handball, futsal, badminton, floorball, tennis and fitness or gymnastics classes. We mark sports lines in any colour configuration.
Does the flooring meet sports federation requirements?+
Yes. Our systems meet the technical parameters required by international sports federations (FIBA, FIVB, FIH). We provide full documentation and certificates required during construction and acceptance of sports halls.
Is the surface resistant to chair marks during events?+
Yes. NS Pro surfaces are resistant to point loads from chairs, stages and event equipment. A sports hall can be used both for games and for concerts, assemblies or large events — without risk of damaging the floor.
